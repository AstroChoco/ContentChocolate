The why, the what and the how

Here presents you my thought-process and skillset for planning this online portfolio in response to the why, the what and the how.

Why I wanted an online presence? To help land me a job as a content designer from hiring managers and recruiters.

What are expected for content designers? Design thinking and competence.

How will I make myself a matching content designer to hiring managers and recruiters? Showing my thought-process by a mindmap, understandings of target audience by user journey and story-telling by a sitemap.
