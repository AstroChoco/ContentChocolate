A content designer who loves content and chocolates

As I’ve mostly worked with instructions and technical docs and signed non-disclosure agreements, I don’t have work samples to present.

So I built this interactive prototype to demo my design thinking process for my online portfolio and branding, while the very same prototype is being implemented.

The content chocolate is far from Forrest Gump’s mysterious box of chocolates—“life was a like a box of chocolates. You never know what you’re gonna get.”

It rather provides a mapping of what you’ll get from the box on the packaging, using evidence and data.
